About ROC

ROC is a non-clinical, recovery-oriented coaching practice focused on motivation, accountability, and practical life change. We support people where they are and help them move forward—without judgment, diagnosis, or pressure.

Services

  • Individual Coaching: $90 per 60-minute session
  • Starter Package: $425 (5 sessions)
  • Momentum Package: $800 (10 sessions)
  • Monthly Support Retainer: $750/month
  • Group Coaching: $40–$60 per person

Our Philosophy

  • Progress matters more than perfection.
  • Structure is a form of care.
  • Recovery is personal and non-linear.
  • Accountability and compassion can coexist.

Contact & Policy

Sessions must be prepaid. 24-hour notice is required for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged in full.

Referral Partners: Courts, clinics, case managers, and community orgs.
